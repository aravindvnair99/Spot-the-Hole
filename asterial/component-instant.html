How do Instants work?

Like Facebook and Instagram when you open an instant article, these elements can open content on the entire size.

How can I deploy Instants?

Instants can be deployed from buttons, cards, articles, link lists, menus and basically everything!

What content can I use?

Any contet that is inside the HTML pages you can add inside the instant elements.
